### AI Impact Analysis Presentation

This presentation was created using Python libraries including **Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn**.  
It was made in **2024** to analyze the potential effects of AI on various industries, evaluating whether disruptions are **long-term** or **short-term trends**.  

The presentation also provides practical recommendations for different audiences:  
- **Employees** facing potential job displacement  
- **AI researchers** seeking career guidance  
- **Individuals** aiming to work in regulated sectors affected by AI
